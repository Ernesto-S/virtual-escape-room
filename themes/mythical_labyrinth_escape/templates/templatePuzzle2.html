Follow the right wall to get out

Story Intro

Freedom at last! Or not. Guess that was too much to hope for, and too easy. As you stand outside the hut you had been trapped in moments ago, you now stare at a wall of hedge-bush, taller than a building and woven tightly together, blocking your path. As you give an exasperated sigh and look imploringly at the fairy, she shoots you a look of disdain. “Don’t think I can fly you over that!” she huffed and wagged a finger at the opening in the wall. “You better start running if you want to make it out of that maze before sundown.” Then she giggled, almost evilly. “You don’t want to be in there after dark!”

Success Story

You collapse to your knees as you stumble out of the maze, sounds of rustling leaves and chittering creatures come from the shadows of the bushes. “Well, safe to say you made it out of there not a moment too soon!” The fairy exclaimed as she floats around you, tsks and shakes her head at the maze. You look up, refusing to glance behind you even though you can feel eyes on your back, and instead, look ahead across an open field of waist high grass, the sun starting to touch the horizon as it sets. Across the field, you spy a silvery gate and jump to your feet with joy – the way out is just ahead! “Well, well, almost there human! Better hurry before the sun sets!” The fairy said, most amused. You take off in a dead run.
