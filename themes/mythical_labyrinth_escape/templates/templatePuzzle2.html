Story Intro

Freedom at last! Or not. Guess that was too much to hope for, and too easy. As you stand outside the hut you had been trapped in moments ago, you now stare at a wall of hedge-bush, taller than a building and woven tightly together, blocking your path. As you give an exasperated sigh and look imploringly at the fairy, she shoots you a look of disdain. “Don’t think I can fly you over that!” she huffed and wagged a finger at the opening in the wall. “You better start running if you want to make it out of that maze before sundown.” Then she giggled, almost evilly. “You don’t want to be in there after dark!”
